WW1 Trench Story

Sound Project

In this project we created a story only told through sound.

The story was to tell about the mortality of soilders on the front line during World War 1. The story focused on the short journey of an individual Who is terrifed and uncertain of what is going to happen to him in the fornt line. The story sadly ends witht the soldiers demise. The main thing of this project is the amazing sound design that brings everyting to life. The sound design is mostly original with voice lines and war sounds recorded by the team and integrated into the sound design.

We Invented A Time Machine

Video Project

In this project we tell a story where its December 2020 and the world is ending. A scientist sends four of his students back in time inorder to stop the approaching doom

In this story we show four different stories where each of the students goes to his past self and tries to warn themselves about the terrible things that are approaching the next year. each of the stories has a distinct storyline and ending. The important focus of this project is the editing and special effects work that has been done to really flesh out the story and make it engaging for the audience to watch. The duel website layout and text also add to the story and adds a duality of reading and watching to the story.
